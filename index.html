ABOUT

Red Apple Apartments (RAA) is an independently minority-owned apartment complex located in the heart of northwest Houston that has served the housing needs for the City of Houston and the Spring Branch District community for over 60 years. Owned by Hector and Josefina, RAA is zoned to Memorial Senior High School, which ranks #9 in Harris County and #29 in the State of Texas.
