Red Apple Apartments (RAA) is a sole-minority-owned apartment complex and small business located along the stretch of Long Point Road adjacent to Spring Branch Creek in the heart of northwest Houston. RAA has served the housing needs for the Spring Branch District for over 55 years, and is currently owned by Hector since 2004. RAA is an active member of the National Apartment Association (NAA), the Texas Apartment Association (TAA), and the Houston Apartment Association (HAA).
